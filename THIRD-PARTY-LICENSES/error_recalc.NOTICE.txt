    - error_recalc: RomHacking.net utility #1264
      https://www.romhacking.net/utilities/1264/

Use in this project: the "error_recalc" executable is invoked as a separate
process to recompute EDC/ECC after the hex writes. This is mere aggregation --
its GPL terms do NOT extend to this project's own (PolyForm Noncommercial) code.
Any release that ships the error_recalc binary must make its corresponding
source available under GPLv3 (GPLv3 section 6).

STATUS: this dependency is slated for removal. The EDC/ECC recompute is being
reimplemented clean-room in pure Python from the ECMA-130 CD-ROM standard (the
EDC CRC and P/Q Reed-Solomon ECC parity), which is not a derivative of this
code. Once that lands, error_recalc.exe and this notice are removed.
